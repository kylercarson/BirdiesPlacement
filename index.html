Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common concerns.

How much does it cost to work with you?

Many placement services are no-cost to families because partner communities may compensate us after move-in. Hospice coordination varies—ask us and we’ll explain clearly.

Do you recommend hospice, or is it only for end-of-life?

Hospice is a specific type of support for people with a serious illness when the focus is comfort and quality of life. We can help you understand eligibility and timing, and what support looks like.

Can you help if we need care quickly?

Yes. If timing is urgent (hospital discharge, safety concern, caregiver burnout), tell us—our process is designed to move fast while keeping decisions thoughtful.
