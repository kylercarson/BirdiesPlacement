About Us

At Birdies Placement, we specialize in connecting seniors and their families with the best senior living communities and hospice care options. Our experienced team understands the unique needs of each individual and works tirelessly to find the perfect placement that ensures comfort, dignity, and quality care.

Our Services

Senior Living Placement

We help families find the ideal senior living communities, including assisted living, independent living, and memory care facilities. Our personalized approach ensures the best match for lifestyle and care needs.

Hospice Placement

Our compassionate team assists in finding appropriate hospice care services, focusing on comfort and support during life's final stages. We coordinate with healthcare providers to ensure seamless transitions.

Consultation & Support

Beyond placement, we offer ongoing consultation and support to families, helping navigate insurance, financing, and care options to make informed decisions.
