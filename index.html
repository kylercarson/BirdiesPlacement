Helpful answers

Frequently asked questions

A few common questions families ask us.

Does Birdie’s provide medical care?

No. Birdie’s Senior Living Solutions is a referral and placement service only. We help families explore options and connect with communities and providers.

Do families pay for your services?

No cost to families. Referral fees are paid by facilities after move-in.

Do you guarantee admission to a facility?

No. We do not guarantee facility admission. Final placement decisions belong to the family, and admissions are determined by each community or provider.

What types of care settings do you help with?

We help families navigate independent living, assisted living, memory care, skilled nursing, hospice, home health, and personal care options.
